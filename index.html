My Projects

Portfolio

Featured here are some of my projects in Data Analysis, NLP & Time-Series Forecasting.

Simple Multivariate Time-Series Forecast

In this project, I build a Vector Autoregression Model to forecast future values of a multivariate time-series. I perform statistical tests on the data to determine things like correlation between variables and stationarity. The entire project is wrapped up with a neat explanation of all steps complete with beautiful Seaborn visualizations in the form of a Medium article.

TF-IDF Matrix for Text Classification

The objective of this project was to classify text. Using NLP techniques to clean the user reviews, I developed a TF-IDF matrix to obtain a importance scores of words in a matrix representation. Built Word Clouds out of different review sources to view the most commonly occuring words.

Analysing News Articles of Strauss-Kahn allegations

I analyzed articles published by leading news sites related to the sexual abuse allegations against Strauss-Kahn, a former IMF director. Using the NLTK library and VADER sentiment analysis tool, I processed the articles obtained quantifiable metrics scores to determine the tone of the articles; positive, negative or neutral.
